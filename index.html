My contributions have been significant in developing the initial version of our site, creating new lessons, and planning innovative features for the upcoming months. Furthermore, I am responsible for promoting the project on social media.

I am always enthusiastic about connecting with like-minded technology enthusiasts. If you'd like to explore potential collaborations, discuss ideas, or connect with me, please do not hesitate to reach out!
